Flying Together


Lets Read

Q1. Where did the geese live?
Ans. The geese lived in a forest on the top of a tall tree.

Q2. Why did the old bird advise the other birds to destroy the creeper?
Ans. The old bird advised the other birds to destroy the creeper because the creeper could help the hunters to climb-up the tree and kill them.

Q3. Why did the geese cry, “Help Help”?
Ans. The geese cried “Help Help” because they were trapped in the net by the hunter.

Q4. What did the hunter do when he thought that the geese were dead?
Ans. The hunter threw the geese out of the net on the ground.
